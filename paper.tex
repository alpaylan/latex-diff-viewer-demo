\begin{abstract}
This short, multi-page paper is a live demonstration of \texttt{latex-diff-viewer}.
Browse the diffs between its commits: added text appears in blue and underlined,
removed text in red and struck through. The viewer also builds a clickable index of
changed pages, so you can jump straight to each edit. Every commit in this repository
produces one such diff.
\end{abstract}
